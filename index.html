MaidCare

Thesis Project

Working as a Project Owner and Front-End Developer (Flutter)

In this Project, me and my team seeing that the neeeds for domestic workers are increasingly demanded given the increasing level of working activity of each person or family. To overcome these problems, families often use the services of domestic workers such as household assistants (ART), Home Care nurses, babysitters, and private drivers. However, finding a trusted domestic worker is not easy because of high prices from domestic worker service providers and negative issues that appears on many news in Indonesia. To overcome this problem we create an app that can facilitating domestic worker search services through digitizing processes, reducing service costs, and participating in improving the welfare of domestic workers.
